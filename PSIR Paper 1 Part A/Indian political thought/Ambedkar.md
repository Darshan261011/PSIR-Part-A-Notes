### On social justice
* Dalits represented The most depressed section of Indian society. Acc to pareto's circulation of elites, power ha traditionally resided between foxes and lions, the upper castes and the OBCs in Indian context, with the dalits constituting the ruled masses.
* mentioned in Manusmriti as antyaj that is outside of Varna system
* Dalits in India suffer from multiple deprivations because of the birth
* Abolition of untouchability and giving life of dignity to untouchables he had first hand experience of the plight of untouchables from his childhood to his adult life when even after graduating in law from Columbia university people did not approach him because of his caste
* For Ambedkar caste is the basic structure of society
* **war of position:** dalitism (counter hegemony)
